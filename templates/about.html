About LEAF DSS

A web-based Decision Support System for planning and evaluating agricultural and livelihood interventions across Assam, India.

What is LEAF DSS?

The Landscape Evaluation & Assessment Framework (LEAF) Decision Support System helps planners identify where specific agricultural and livelihood interventions are most likely to succeed. It combines block- and Gram-Panchayat-level data on land, water, infrastructure, livestock, soil, climate and demographics into a single interactive map and scoring engine.

Users select an intervention, adjust the variables and thresholds that matter, and the system scores each area for feasibility — turning scattered datasets into a clear, comparable picture of where to act.

What it does

Geospatial Visualization

Interactive maps of block and GP data across all districts, with district boundaries and protected-area overlays.

Multi-Criteria Feasibility Scoring

A weighted scoring engine evaluates each area against configurable variable ranges and weights.

Cluster Planning

Groups nearby villages into intervention clusters based on member counts and proximity, with CSV download/upload.

AI-Powered Recommendations

Combines area metrics with policy documents to generate citation-backed, actionable recommendations.

Data & Maintenance

Intervention configurations and variable values are managed through linked Google Sheets and refreshed into the dashboard on demand — see the Data Update page. Geospatial boundaries and the AI knowledge base are maintained by the development team.
